Notes

Templates for lecture notes, reading notes, and proof notebooks.

Lecture (Outline)

  • Header: date, topic, reading pages.
  • Left margin for key terms; right for follow-ups.
  • End with 3 questions to test recall.

Cornell Lite

  • Notes on the right, cues on the left.
  • Summary in 3 sentences at the bottom.
  • Highlight at most 3 lines per page.

Proof Notebook

  • Use a wide left margin for structure (claims, lemmas).
  • State goal in one sentence before the algebra.
  • Tag unclear steps for a follow-up pass.
